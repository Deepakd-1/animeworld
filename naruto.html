Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the village leader. The story is divided into two parts -- the first set in Naruto's pre-teen years, and the second in his teens. The manga has been adapted into an anime television series produced by Studio Pierrot and Aniplex.
