One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since 1997. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit, as he searches for the legendary treasure known as "One Piece" and pursues his dream to become the Pirate King.
