Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy whose family is slaughtered by demons, and his journey to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister Nezuko, who was transformed into a demon.
